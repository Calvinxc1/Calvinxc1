# Research Base Module

Use this module for repositories centered on experiments, scientific modeling,
literature review, or research documentation.

## Research Artifact Roles

- Treat literature reviews as source-backed synthesis, notes as framed
  interpretation or hypothesis work, and notebooks as prototype-specific
  commentary tied to actual experiments or diagnostics.
- Do not force literature reviews, notes, and notebooks into the same voice or
  burden them with the same purpose.
- Keep topic `README.md` files as orientation documents rather than miniature
  literature reviews.

## Structural Guidance

- Keep shared research-process documents at the `research/` root when the
  repository materially includes research work.
- Keep topic materials in dedicated topic folders rather than scattering them
  across the repository.
- Pair this module with the research structure, citations, framing, and review
  modules when a repository needs more specific research-policy segmentation.
